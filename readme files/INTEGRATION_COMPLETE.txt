📍 HOW TO USE:

1. Open the app (npm start)
2. Go to TOPICS or create a new document
3. Write your worldbuilding content and notes
4. Click the "🔍 Auto-Scan" button in the editor toolbar (gold button)
5. Choose: AI-enhanced or Rule-based scanning
6. Review the results in the popup modal
7. Check/uncheck suggestions you want to add
8. Click "Apply All Checked" or add items individually

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✨ WHAT IT DOES:

When you scan a document, it will:
  ✓ Extract character names with titles (King, Queen, Lord, etc.)
  ✓ Detect timeline events with dates
  ✓ Identify themes/topics (Magic, War, Politics, etc.)
  ✓ Find location names
  ✓ Match existing vault entries (suggests links)
  ✓ Show confidence scores for each suggestion

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 EXAMPLE WORKFLOW:

1. Write about your world:
   ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │ In the year 1450, King Aldric led his forces against    │
   │ the dark sorcerer Malachar. The Battle of Thornfield    │
   │ would decide the fate of the Kingdom of Eredane...      │
   └──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

2. Click "🔍 Auto-Scan"

3. Get results:
   📝 New Characters:
      ✓ King Aldric (Male, 90% confidence)
      ✓ Malachar (50% confidence)
   
   📅 New Events:
      ✓ Battle of Thornfield [1450]
   
   🏷️ New Topics:
      ✓ War (8 mentions)
      ✓ Magic (3 mentions)
   
   🗺️ Locations:
      ✓ Kingdom of Eredane

4. Click checkboxes to select what you want
5. Click "Apply All Checked"
6. Done! Entities are now in your vault
