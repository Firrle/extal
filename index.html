Travel Time Measurement Setup
Follow route is best for roads/trails with turns. Direct line is best for "as the crow flies" estimates.
Use Standard for normal journeys, Careful for wagons/rough routes, and Forced only for short bursts.
Enter a manual mounted pace, or enable auto-calculate.
Enter how many map units a traveler covers in 1 day. Real-world baseline preset uses about 1.75x mounted pace vs walking. Results are shown as weeks, days, hours, and minutes.
Measure a route first, then enter how long that trip is known to take.
Use Measure mode to click at least 2 points, then enter the trip duration here.
Calibration route: select at least 2 map points in Measure mode.
